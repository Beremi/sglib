sglib by ezander

A Malab/Octave toolbox for stochastic Galerkin methods

SGLib is a toolbox for Matlab helping in the development of stochastic Galerkin and (to a lesser part as yet) stochastic collocation methods. More or less extensive documentation is available in toolbox itself.
